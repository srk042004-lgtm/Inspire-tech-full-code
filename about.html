Diploma in Information Technology (DIT)

DIT is a **1-year professional diploma** that covers almost every major IT subject. Students will learn about computer hardware, software, programming fundamentals (C/C++/Java basics), networking, and databases. It is a full package for those who want to build a strong foundation in the IT field.

Scope: DIT is highly recognized in Pakistan and Gulf countries. It can help you get government and private IT-related jobs.

  • Duration: 1 Year
  • Subjects: Computer Fundamentals, Programming, Databases, Networking
  • Eligibility: Matric / Intermediate Students
  • Diploma Certificate Provided

Certificate in Information Technology (CIT)

CIT is a **6-month short IT course** for beginners. It focuses on computer basics, MS Office, Internet usage, email handling, and an introduction to graphics and basic coding. This course is perfect for students, office workers, and professionals who want to improve their computer knowledge quickly.

Scope: Students who complete CIT can easily apply for clerical jobs, office jobs, and data entry positions.

  • Duration: 6 Months
  • Includes: Computer Basics, MS Office, Internet, Intro to IT
  • Certificate Provided

MS Office

This course teaches Microsoft Office applications including **Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Access**. These tools are essential for office work, creating professional documents, spreadsheets, presentations, and databases.

Scope: Every office job requires MS Office skills, making this course a must for students and job seekers.

  • Duration: 3 Months
  • Level: Beginner to Intermediate
  • Hands-on Projects Included
  • Certificate Provided

Typing (English & Urdu)

This course is focused on **improving typing speed and accuracy** in both English and Urdu. Students will learn touch typing techniques, correct hand placement, and typing software usage. Daily practice sessions will ensure continuous improvement in speed.

Scope: Useful for clerical jobs, data entry, and students preparing for exams requiring typing.

  • Duration: 2 Months
  • Typing Tests & Daily Practice
  • Certificate Provided
